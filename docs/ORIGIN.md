# Origin

Booking recovery is not just a support problem. It is an operations, inventory, payment, and communication problem. The moment a stay, itinerary, or room block changes, teams need a surface that maps disruption pressure to recovery evidence and the next safe move.

We built `booking-disruption-command-center` to make that operating layer explicit. The point is not to replace a PMS, CRS, or guest messaging tool. The point is to show what a Hospitality / Travel Tech operator surface should look like when the audience needs to manage disruption pressure, downstream blockers, and guest-facing recovery posture with real revenue consequences attached.
